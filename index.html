Accept payments from any channel through a single Transfyn integration — with real-time reporting, automatic reconciliation, and T+1 settlement to your bank.

Send money to hundreds of recipients at once — salaries, vendor payments, refunds, commissions, and marketplace payouts — from a single API call.

  • Marketplace seller payouts
  • Full audit trail per disbursement

FAQ
Questions,
answered plainly.

The questions we are asked most often. If yours isn't here, reach out — we respond within 24 hours.

How long does a typical web or app project take?

A Starter Website takes 2–4 weeks. A Business Platform 6–10 weeks. Enterprise and SaaS projects run 12–24 weeks depending on complexity. A detailed project schedule is provided during the scoping call before any commitment is made.

Can Transfyn be integrated without a Grimm Technologies build?

Yes. Transfyn is available as a standalone product with its own REST API and developer documentation. Any development team can integrate it into an existing platform. Clients who also engage Grimm for development receive Transfyn at preferential commercial rates.

Are the prices on the pricing page fixed rates?

The rates shown are starting prices for the described scope. Every engagement is scoped and priced individually after a short discovery conversation. We provide a fixed-price proposal — there is no hourly billing and no surprise invoices.

What is included in post-launch support?

Post-launch support includes bug resolution, minor content updates, performance monitoring, and technical assistance. Duration and scope depend on your package. For ongoing feature development, we offer monthly retainer arrangements.

Do you work with clients outside Pakistan?

Yes. We work with clients in the UAE, UK, Saudi Arabia, and other markets — particularly businesses with Pakistan-facing digital operations or those seeking quality engineering at competitive rates. All engagement is remote-friendly.

Is Transfyn PCI DSS certified?

Yes. Transfyn is PCI DSS certified and applies AML/KYC controls on all transactions. The platform uses AES-256 encryption and TLS 1.3 in transit. For specific compliance or regulatory enquiries, please contact our team directly.
